**1. Equity-credit sequencing "83% hit rate" as a statistical base rate**
- **Killed by:** quant_researcher_01 (Claim 5)
- **Reasoning:** Binomial test of 5/6 against 50% null yields p=0.11 — fails conventional significance. 3/3 "well-documented episodes" is even weaker (p=0.125). Episode selection is researcher-defined. pair_2 debate called this "dispositive." ## KEY_DISAGREEMENTS

**1. Whether the research process exhibits systematic bearish bias or appropriate risk-weighting**
- **Parties:** challenger_02 (14 bearish vs. 4 bullish mechanisms = reflexive contrarianism) vs. implicit framework of all other agents
- **Status:** challenger_02's mechanism count is factual. But pair_3 debate noted a building can fail in hundreds of ways and only needs to stand up in one — failure-mode asymmetry is structural, not evidence of analytical bias. The disagreement is unresolvable without a probability-weighted expected return framework that aggregates all mechanisms — which *no agent attempts*. This is the deepest gap in the entire research process: increasingly sophisticated risk identification without ever answering "given all of this, what should you own?"

**2. Whether the catalyst for cycle turn is a financial ratio threshold or an external revenue signal**
- **Parties:** generalist_01 / equity_analyst_01 (dual threshold: ROIC < WACC for 4+ quarters AND capex/OCF > 55-65%) vs. generalist_02 (shale shows external price signal was binding, not financial ratios)
- **Status:** pair_0 debate concluded both may be necessary, neither sufficient: the ratio crossing creates *conditions* for a revenue disappointment to be interpreted as thesis failure rather than temporary miss. The specific thresholds (55-65%, 4 quarters) are refuted, but the concept of a financial-conditions-as-predisposition plus external-catalyst-as-trigger framework deserves development.

**3. Primary crash transmission channel: basis trade vs. international flow reversal**
- **Parties:** generalist_01 (basis trade $800B-$1T at 50-100x leverage) vs. generalist_02 (international flow reversal $300-500B)
- **Status:** pair_0 debate concluded neither dominates; the combination is worse than either alone. The concerning scenario — joint activation where revenue disappointment triggers equity selloff, foreign outflows, dollar decline, basis trade margin pressure, Treasury dislocation — is multiplicative, not additive. Neither agent fully articulates this interaction. This is the most dangerous unmodeled tail risk.

**4. Statistical reliability vs. operational utility of working estimates**
- **Parties:** quant_researcher_01 (most confidence ratings are fictitious given small N) vs. equity_analyst_01 (directional trades require working estimates even with wide CIs)
- **Status:** Genuinely unresolvable methodological tension. pair_2 debate concluded the optimal output is equity_analyst_01's framework with quant_researcher_01's calibration imposed: most directional trades are sound, but position sizing should reflect far wider uncertainty bands. The research process needs to formally distinguish "analytical conviction" from "statistical reliability" in every confidence rating.

**5. Whether passive indexation (55% of AUM) makes unwinding slower or more violent**
- **Parties:** generalist_02 raises this as a structural unknown; no agent resolves it
- **Status:** No analogue in any agent's library has ~55% passive during an unwinding phase (2000 was ~15-20%). The 2022 drawdown (~25-30%) was not accompanied by fundamental narrative fracture, making it an imperfect test. This first-order uncertainty affects every position-sizing decision and has zero empirical calibration.

**6. Whether the winner-take-all equilibrium scenario deserves more weight**
- **Parties:** challenger_02 (under-explored because analytically unsatisfying to all camps) vs. generalist_01 (fragile partial reconciliation at confidence 6.5)
- **Status:** The scenario where bears are right about broad economy (no TFP), bulls are right about specific companies (value capture), and rates are right (no r* shift) denies clean narratives to every analytical perspective. challenger_02 argues this is exactly why it may deserve more weight. Currently under-explored relative to its probability.

- **Agents:** challenger_02 (logical contradiction) vs risk_analyst_01 / generalist_02 (both invoke historical patterns while noting passive structural break)
- **Stakes:** The entire KB architecture rests on this question. Every confidence rating that depends on a historical base rate should be accompanied by an explicit structural-uncertainty discount.
- **For future research:** Propose a formal protocol for adjusting base-rate-derived confidence ratings given passive investing's 55% share.

- **Agents:** commodities_analyst_01 (leading — expect propagation to large caps in 2-4 quarters) vs macro_strategist_01 (lagging — peak cumulative tightening effect approaching, may stabilize)
- **Stakes:** If leading, de-risk; if lagging, stay invested. Both channels are likely operative simultaneously.
- **For future research:** Monitor whether small-cap margins continue deepening after Fed holds/eases (validates input-cost thesis) or stabilize (validates transmission-lag thesis). macro_strategist_01's hypothesis that large-cap/small-cap divergence reflects monetary transmission asymmetry by funding structure (bond vs bank) deserves direct testing.

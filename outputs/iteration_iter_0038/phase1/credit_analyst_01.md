3. **What is the current CCC-bucket utilization across the CLO market?** This is the most operationally critical unknown. 8. **At what point does the carry trap become a measurably bad risk-reward?** The payoff asymmetry (narrowing carry, widening tail risk) is logically established, but translating this into a specific spread level or date for portfolio action requires quantifying the tail — which is fundamentally unquantifiable under Knightian uncertainty (n=1 for basis trade crystallization, n=0 for the current US fiscal configuration).
